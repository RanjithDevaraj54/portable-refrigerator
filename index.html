Portable Smart Mini Refrigerator
For future environment by not producing any harmful gases

Our Motivation

WE PREFER COLD

More people prefer to drink juices and water in chill condition while travelling.So we came to solve thier problems.

FOR LOVED ONE

Person suffer from diabetes.They need to carry their insulin which has to be maintained in certain cold conditions.

TO CARRY

In such case we need to carry these things in a smart and portable equipment in certain cold temperature.

USE IT EVERYWHERE

And it can be used everywhere using electricity and also by solar energy to keep the things in chill condition

Our Objective

NO CFC

This technique provides the solution for the emission of Chlorofluorocarbons [CFC] from normal refrigerators and stand Unique

EFFICIENCY

This equipment provides improved efficiency compared to all the normal refrigerator available in market today as best sellers

SMART

Smart and Portable refrigerators can be used to carry our own foods and medicines to the desired place at desired level of temperature

PRODUCT

This system can be enlarged to the commercial product and used in certain conditions like travelling and also for storing medicines.
